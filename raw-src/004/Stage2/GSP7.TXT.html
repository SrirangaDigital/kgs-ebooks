I find solace in Bhagavad Geetha that I miss even in the Sermon on the Mount. When disappointment stares me in the face and all alone I see not one ray of light, I go back to Bhagavad Geetha. I find a verse here and a verse there and I immediately begin to smile in the midst of overwhelming tragedies; and my life has been full of external tragedies. And if they have left no visible or indelible scar on me, I owe it all to the teachings of the Bhagavad Geetha.
