I find solace in ್ಝಘ್ಒಗ್ಒಡ್ಒಕ್ಡ್ಓಓಠ್ಘ್ಒ that I miss even in the Sermon on the Mount. When disappointment stares me in the face and all alone I see not one ray of light, I go back to ್ಝಘ್ಒಗ್ಒಡ್ಒಕ್ಡ್ಓಓಠ್ಘ್ಒ.
